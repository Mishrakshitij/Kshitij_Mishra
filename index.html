About Me

I am a researcher specializing in Conversational AI, LLM editing methods, and privacy-preserving AI. My work focuses on developing AI systems that exhibit politeness, empathy, and logical reasoning in diverse domains. [cite: 9]

Download CV | Download Research Statement

Research

My research explores:

  • Conversational AI systems for counseling, healthcare, and assistance. [cite: 4, 5]
  • Parameter-efficient LLM editing using methods like Salient Layer Editing. [cite: 2, 16]
  • Privacy-preserving large language models. [cite: 3]
  • Logical reasoning and bias mitigation in AI applications.
  • Inducing Politeness, Empathy, Personalization, Persuasiveness, and Counseling in Conversational AI Systems. [cite: 9]
