About Me

I am a researcher specializing in Conversational AI, LLM editing methods, and privacy-preserving AI. My work focuses on developing AI systems that exhibit politeness, empathy, and logical reasoning in diverse domains.

Research

My research explores:

  • Conversational AI systems for counseling, healthcare, and assistance.
  • Parameter-efficient LLM editing using methods like Salient Layer Editing.
  • Logical reasoning and bias mitigation in AI applications.
